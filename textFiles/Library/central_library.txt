<p>
    The Library of the university was established in 1964 and it is one of the first department started in the University. It is regarded as one of the best
    library in the Malwa region of MP. Apart from the Central library the university has strong departmental libraries also.
</p>
<div>
    <br/>
</div>
<div>
    <strong>Mission</strong>
</div>
<div>
    The primary mission of the library is to support the educational and research programmes of the university by providing physical and intellectual access to
    information, consistent with the present and the anticipated educational and research functions of the university.
    <br/>
</div>
<div>
    The secondary mission is, to serve as a resource center for the scholars and scientific community of the Malwa region. The collection of the library which
    includes books, journals, reports, theses is regarded one of the richest collections in the region.
    <br/>
</div>
<div>
    <br/>
</div>
<div>
    The library currently receives over 200 periodical titles
    <br/>
</div>

    <strong>Automation of Library</strong>
</div>
<div>
    <strong>
        <br/>
    </strong>
</div>
<div>
    The library has been using the SOUL an Integrated Library Management software package with all the modules for the library housekeeping operations. The
    library Online Catalogue database has more than 30 thousand records of books, which is under continuous updation. Using SOUL OPAC, users can search the
    Library Online Catalog by Author, Title, Subject, and keywords in the library. The same library catalog is available for the members on intranet in the
    campus.

    <br/>

    provided for the members only.
</p>
<p align="justify">
   <b> Reference Services<a name="rs"></b></a>
    <br/>
    Library staff are available in the reading room to suggest sources of information and to assist the members in locating the required material. Only a
    skeleton staff is available after normal working hours . Hence at normal working hours the senior staff on duty at the help desk may be approached for any
    help in the location of the required documents.
</p>
