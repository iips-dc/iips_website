<b>Class rooms & Lecture Theatres</b><br>

   The lush green campus of IIPS includes an academic complex of classroom, seminar
   room, an auditorium, a well equipped library, computer center and administrative 
offices. the institute has two air conditioned, well equipped lecture theatres which
 are used for seminars and lectures. These lectures have played host to some of the 
most well known personalities of the corporate world.
